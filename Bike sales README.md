# Bike Sales and Customer Data Analysis

## Project overview:

This project analyses customer demographic and lifestyle data to identify factors that may influence bike purchase behaviour. The project involved cleaning and transforming raw customer data in Excel, creating pivot tables for analysis, and building an interactive dashboard to visualise trends and customer patterns.  
The goal of the project was to explore relationships between different customer demographics and bike purchase decisions.

## Tools used:
Excel

## Skills:
- Data cleaning  
- Data preparation  
- Excel formulas  
- Pivot tables  
- Data analysis  
- Excel dashboard creation  
- Slicers  

## Analysis:
- Bike purchase outcome based on the customer income by gender.  
- Count of purchased bikes of male and female customers by age category.  
- Count of bikes purchased based on commute distance customers had to travel for the purchase.  

## Key findings:
- Both male and female customers that had higher income were more likely to purchase a bike. Male customers were more likely to purchase a bike than their female counterparts.

- Both male and female customers in the middle-aged category had the highest count of purchased bikes. Customers in the adolescent and elderly category brought bikes significantly less.

- The count of bikes purchased by customers decrease with commuting distance required to purchase the bike. One way this can be tackled with is investing into delivery options for customers who are interested in purchasing the bike but don’t have the means to travel for the purchase.
